About Us

Our Mission

Hiraya is an interactive wellness platform designed to help users overcome challenges and improve their overall health and well-being. Our web application guides users through personalized solutions to address their existing wellness issues while tracking their progress over time.

What We Offer: Daily and weekly challenges including physical activities, mental exercises, and social activities. Users earn badges for completing challenges, can share their wellness journey with friends, and receive support through our community features.

We recognize the alarming increase in mental health issues, particularly among students. Hiraya addresses the critical need for accessible wellness support, providing a safe space for users to improve their physical, mental, emotional, and social well-being without the barriers that often prevent people from seeking help.

Our platform performs daily check-ins and provides personalized tasks tailored to each user's individual challenges, creating a supportive environment for healing and growth.
